GETTING THE MOST OUT OF YOUR SESSION

It is common for those new to therapy to feel both eager to begin, and uncomfortable about beginning the process of therapy. We encourage you to not let any feelings of awkwardness keep you from beginning what you know will be in your long-term best interest. If you have questions and require answers before this appointment, please call or email us so that we can discuss these. As with most situations in life, you will likely find that as you forge ahead, your worries will quickly lessen. Over the years, our clients have found it very helpful to think about what they want to get from treatment or therapy. Please make some notes about your goals and what is most important to you, so that this can be discussed when we meet.

As we embark on this journey together, our pledge to you is to handle all communications and interactions between us with confidentiality, respect, and sensitivity. We see our relationship with you as a partnership, and we are committed to providing you with safe, compassionate, and highly-skilled care.
